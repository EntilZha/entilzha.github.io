I work on Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing with Jordan Boyd-Graber, and others in the CLIP lab. I am interested in knowledge grounded natural language understanding, question answering, few-shot learning, domain adaptation, and interpretability. I led the development of an AI that defeated some of the best Quiz Bowl players in the world. Visit qanta.org to learn more about our datasets, systems, and research.

This summer (2019) I will be a research intern at Facebook AI Research. I have worked as a research scientist or data scientist at Microsoft Research - Information and Data Science Group, Riot Games, Zillow Group at Trulia, Oracle Datalogix, UC Berkeley's AMPLab, UC Berkeley's Astronomy Department, and Boise State University's Cryosphere Group.
